Weekend Nature Escape

🌿 DEVKUND ROUTE

Satara → Bhira → Devkund → Tamhini Ghat → Pune
📍 Total: ~200km | ⏱️ 2 Days Perfect
📅 SATURDAY
🚗 Satara → Bhira (2.5hrs)
🥾 Trek to Devkund (4km)
🏊 Swim in emerald pool
🏠 Stay at Bhira village
📅 SUNDAY
🌅 Morning trek completion
🛣️ Scenic Tamhini Ghat drive
📸 Valley viewpoints
🏙️ Reach Pune by evening

🌟 Why This Route?

• Completely untouched & unexplored
• Swimming in pristine emerald pools
• Most scenic drive in Maharashtra
• Less crowded, authentic experience

💧 THOSEGHAR ROUTE

📍 Total: ~150km | ⏱️ Relaxed Trip
📅 SATURDAY
🚗 Satara → Thoseghar (26km)
🏆 #1 Attraction in Satara
💧 Series of waterfalls
🏠 Stay near Thoseghar
📅 SUNDAY
🌸 Kaas Plateau (Valley of Flowers)
🛣️ Western Ghats scenic drive
📸 Flower photography

🌟 Why This Route?

• Easy access & less trekking
• Famous waterfall destination
• Seasonal flowers (Aug-Oct)
• Good for all fitness levels

🎯 ADVENTURE COMBO

Satara → Thoseghar → Bhira → Devkund → Pune
📍 Total: ~250km | ⏱️ Action-Packed
📅 SATURDAY
🌅 Morning: Thoseghar Falls
🚗 Afternoon: Drive to Bhira
🏠 Evening: Bhira village stay
🍽️ Local food experience
📅 SUNDAY
🥾 Early morning: Devkund trek
🏊 Swim in emerald pool
🛣️ Tamhini Ghat drive
🏙️ Evening: Pune arrival

🌟 Why This Route?

• Best of both worlds
• Maximum waterfall experience
• Adventure + relaxation
• For energetic travelers

🎒 Essential Tips

  • Start early both days (7 AM recommended)
  • Carry rain gear & trekking shoes
  • Book Bhira homestays in advance
  • Best time: July-December
  • Moderate fitness needed for Devkund
  • Carry snacks & water for treks
